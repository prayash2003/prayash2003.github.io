Who I Am

A lawyer who doesn't
wait to practise law.

I am a BA LLB candidate at Rajarshi Janak University, Janakpurdham (Batch 2022), achieving a First Division CGPA of 3.00 / 4.00. But what defines me is not a grade — it is action taken before graduation.

"The courtroom is not just a room in a building. It is everywhere accountability is demanded."

While still a student, I filed Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) requests to the US Air Force and US Indo-Pacific Command (INDOPACOM) and received official correspondence from their military headquarters in Hawaii — making me likely the first Nepali law student to do so.

I filed an official CIAA complaint against my own university for violations of Bar Council Rules, covered by three national newspapers. I submitted memoranda to the Prime Minister's Office and published 3-part investigative journalism in national media.

I believe a lawyer's duty begins not at the Bar — but the moment injustice can be challenged.
